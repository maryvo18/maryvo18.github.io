Introduction to Literature

One of the biggest issues with the next generations is the lack of emphasis on literacy. This is an issue in our communities, across the state, country, and even the world. The ENGL 3844 group Leading in Literature has provided information about literacy for the public.

Statistics

There are many alarming statistics of people who are illiterate. Here are a few that really make a difference:

  • 10% Adults in Montgomery County, Virginia
  • 12% Adults across the state of Virginia
  • 14% Adults across the country
  • 13.7% People above the age of 15 across the world

Some Background on the Project

Leading in Literature is a group that was formed in the ENGL 3844 class of the spring 2017 semester. We were tasked with coming up with a service-learning project for a cause we felt was important. Literacy is an issue that effects everyone worldwide, so we felt bringing this issue to the forefront of people's minds was going to be successful.
